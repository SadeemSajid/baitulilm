Revive The Traditions of Islamic Academics.

The House of Knowledge (بيت العلم) works to bring about Islamic revivalism through the prevalence of academic content and connecting scholars with the general populace.

Founder's Note:

The current state of affairs within Pakistan are enough to understand that the masses have neglected Islam. Their idea of a religion now only involves the basic five pillars of Islam. Contrastingly, the Companions never had this idea. For them, Islam was a complete philosophy as well as practise. In addition to this, a number of innovations have crept into the lives of the general populace.

This is where Bait Ul-Ilm (The House of Knowledge) comes in. This is a venture to provide scholarly content to the masses to empower them intellectually, remove innovations, and re-establish the correct applications of Islam in their lives. This will in turn remove doubts, and as the Qur'an says, turn their faces fully towards this religion of Allah, so that it may triumph if Allah wills.

~ Sadeem Sajid

The project is a work in progress.
